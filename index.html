OUR JOURNEY

The Story of Shree Bala Ji

From 10 Quilts to a Trusted Legacy

In 2011, with nothing but determination and 10 rajai (quilts), Mr. Hansraj began his journey in the heart of Bhiwani's Haluwas Gate. The journey was not easy — built on late nights, honest dealings, and deep commitment to customer satisfaction.

Over the years, word spread across Bhiwani. Families began trusting Shree Bala Ji for their most cherished occasions — weddings, religious gatherings, and government events. Each rental was a promise of quality, warmth, and reliability.

"Every quilt we rented was a promise — a promise of warmth, trust, and reliability. That promise has never changed in 14 years."— Mr. Hansraj, Founder

Today, under the co-leadership of Mr. Aryan, Shree Bala Ji continues to grow — bringing modern booking systems and the same founding values of integrity and hard work.

Our Story of Trust & Dedication

Shree Bala Ji Bister House was founded in 2011 by Mr. Hansraj with a simple mission — provide clean, affordable rental bedding and tent solutions to the people of Bhiwani. Starting from Haluwas Gate with minimal resources, his commitment to quality and fair pricing quickly built a loyal customer base.

Over 14 years, we have grown to become Bhiwani's most reliable rental service, catering to thousands of families, government organizations, wedding planners, and event managers across Haryana.

Under the co-leadership of Mr. Aryan, we continue expanding with digital bookings, faster delivery, and an ever-growing product range.
